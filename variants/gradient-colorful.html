Building Agentic AI

Architect of enterprise agentic AI systems with over 18 years of experience designing autonomous, multi-agent solutions that transform how organizations operate. I specialize in building AI agents using Azure AI Foundry, Semantic Kernel, and Copilot Studio—leveraging retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), tool-calling, function calling, and multi-agent orchestration to deliver intelligent, context-aware automation at scale.

Beyond AI, I bring deep expertise in Azure cloud architecture, Microsoft 365, and Power Platform—ensuring AI solutions are production-grade, governed, secure, and integrated into existing enterprise ecosystems. As a technical leader, I mentor engineering teams, drive architecture reviews, and bridge the gap between AI innovation and business strategy.
